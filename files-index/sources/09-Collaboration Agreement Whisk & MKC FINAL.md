                                             Recitals

        WHEREAS, McCormick owns and operates an application and an application
programming interface and profiling service associating food preferences and requirements with
recipes and ingredient products in terms of behavioral analytics and insights associated with food
and the eating experience lifecycle (“FlavorPrint Services”);

        WHEREAS, Whisk owns and operates an application and an application programming
interface that enables users to create shopping lists, both from recipes and from scratch, purchase
the ingredients within a shopping list through selected retailers, and search and bookmark recipes
("Whisk Offerings");

        WHEREAS, McCormick and Whisk desire to enter into a strategic business
relationship (the “Collaboration") pursuant to which McCormick will use its knowledge of the
consumer foods business, its research on consumer behavior, its relationships with retailers and
its brand spending to enable Whisk to expand the Whisk Offerings and Whisk will use its
knowledge, know-how and various other capabilities to assist McCormick in accelerating
McCormick's deployment of FlavorPrint Services on select McCormick controlled web
properties outside of the United States;

        NOW THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

       1.   Whisk Services. Whisk will perform the following services in furtherance of
McCormick's business and expansion of the FlavorPrint Services (collectively, "Whisk
Services"):

        (a)    Analysis Service. Whisk shall leverage Whisk's software, know-how and other
technology, but excluding any necessary hardware, to provide Analysis Service (as defined
below) to McCormick. "Analysis Service" means the semantic analysis of McCormick’s recipes
provided by McCormick (“Recipes”) as described in Part 1 of Exhibit E to conform to localized
and canonical concepts identified by Whisk, after liasing with McCormick in respect of the
same, in the "Core Relevant Markets" (as identified in Exhibit A hereto). The work product
resulting from Whisk's Analysis Service (“Work Product”) shall take the form set out in Part 2 of
Exhibit E.
Whisk shall commence providing Analysis Services for the "Additional Potential Markets"
identified in Exhibit B from and after the date McCormick provides the financing to Whisk in
accordance with the Note (as hereinafter defined).

        (b)    Consulting and Analytics Services. Whisk shall also provide consulting and
analytics services pertaining to the Core Relevant Markets and the Recipes which shall take the
form of analysing each Recipe to identify the ingredients and providing one or more lists of the
ingredients used in recipes in each relevant country in such an order that prioritises the most
important ingredients (as determined by Whisk) in the single most spoken language in each of
the Core Relevant Markets.

        (c)    Promotion Services. The parties shall agree from time to time the use by Whisk
of various promotion techniques to drive users and consumers to sign up for FlavorPrint Services
residing on McCormick's web properties, including without limitation banner and other
advertising, pursuant to the cost model set forth on Exhibit D.

        (d)    Default Product and Advertising. Whisk shall provide McCormick with
privileged default positions in shopping lists on Whisk’s app and website in all McCormick
categories at McCormick’s cost in accordance with the cost model set out in Exhibit D.
Additionally, Whisk shall provide McCormick with preferential advertising rates in accordance
with the cost model contained in Exhibit D.

        (e)    Analytical Data. Whisk will provide McCormick with anonymized analytical
data concerning the preferential advertising described above in line with Whisk's standard
advertising analyatics.

        (f)    Driving Scale. Whisk will provide McCormick with advice, counsel and
assistance on an ongoing basis in driving scale to acquire new FlavorPrint Service users in its
Core Relevant Markets and as agreed in Additional Potential Markets.

        (g)     For the avoidance of doubt. Notwithstanding anything to the contrary contained
in this Agreement or otherwise, Whisk shall not be required to share any data of any kind
relating to the use of Whisk’s service by any of Whisk’s customers that Whisk considers are in
competition with McCormick.

        2.    McCormick Services. McCormick will perform the following services in
furtherance of Whisk's business and expansion of the Whisk Offerings (collectively,
"McCormick Services")

       (a)     Integration of Whisk Offerings. McCormick shall integrate Whisk Offerings,
       including recipe selection, shopping lists, shopping trip planning/execution,
       personalization and all other services associated with the use of the Whisk Offerings (as
       currently performed on www.schwartz.co.uk and as may be performed in the future) into
       www.ducros.fr, www.kamis.pl and other websites owned or controlled by McCormick, as
       agreed between the parties from time to time.

       (b)   Driving Scale. McCormick shall provide advice, counsel and assistance to
       Whisk in driving scale to acquire new Whisk Offerings users as a lead partner in select

Exhibit E - Part 1

Whisk will use its natural language processing technology in order to extract the following
information from each recipe line written in the Recipes passed to Whisk by McCormick for
processing:
            Amount.
            Canonicalised Ingredient.
            Ingredient Instructions, e.g. Brand, Cooking/Preparation instruction for the
               ingredient that is included in the recipe’s ingredient list.

Whisk also links each Canonicalised Ingredient to a Unique Global ID that exists for each
Canonicalised Ingredient across different languages.

Exhibit E – Part 2

Where Whisk has been able to successfully process the data as set out in Part 1 above, Whisk
agrees to make this processed data (the Work Product) available to McCormick only via an
Application Programming Interface (API). The Work Product accessible via the API, will
include:
           a. Amount
           b. Canonicalised Ingredient + Unique Global ID
           c. Ingredient Instruction

The scope of Work Product may change from time to time as agreed by both parties.
