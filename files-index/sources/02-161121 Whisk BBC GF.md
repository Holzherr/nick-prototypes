# Interpretation

## The definitions and rules of interpretation in this clause apply in this licence (including the Term Sheet).

**Brand Revenue:** the Revenue received by Whisk from Product Brand
Sponsors in relation to advertisements displayed through the Widget.

**Break Date:** as set out in the Term Sheet (if any).

**Button:** the pictorial part of the Widget shown on the Sites and in a
form and containing words and advertisements to be determined by Whisk.

**Confidential information:** has the meaning given in clause 10.1.

**Content:** all Recipes (whether comprising text, information, data,
images, audio or video material or otherwise) in whatever medium or form
and whether provided to Whisk by the Licensor for incorporation in the
Platform or displayed on the Site and linked to the Platform by way of
the Button.

**Delivery Date(s):** the date(s) specified in the Term Sheet.

**Effective Date:** as set out in the Term Sheet.

**Platform Fees:** The fees payable by the Licensor in respect of the
platform.

**Revenue Fees:** The fees payable in respect of affiliate revenue
earned from supermarkets.

**Force Majeure Event:** has the meaning given in clause 9.1.

**Initial Term:** as set out in the Term Sheet.

**Intellectual Property Rights:** all intellectual property rights
wherever in the world arising, whether registered or unregistered (and
including any application), including copyright, know-how, confidential
information, trade secrets, business names and domain names, Marks,
patents, utility models, design rights, database rights and all rights
in the nature of unfair competition rights or rights to sue for passing
off.

**Marks:** any and all trade marks, trade names, service marks, trade
dress, logos, URLs or identifying slogans of a party to this licence,
whether or not registered.

**Payment Term:** the payment period for the Fee, as set out in the Term
Sheet.

**Platform:** the Whisk platform comprising the Whisk mobile
applications, mobile application and web browser plug-in, the Widget and
the website at www.whisk.com (or such other websites as Whisk shall
notify to the Licensor from time to time), and such other elements as
Whisk may develop or add from time to time.

**Product Brand Sponsors:** those brand sponsors of either Whisk or the
Licensor whose products are subject to particular advertising placement
by agreement with Whisk or Licensor (as applicable).

**Purchase:** a purchase of ingredients for a Recipe by a User from a
Retailer.

**Recipe:** a culinary recipe, whether a mere list of ingredients or
including explanatory instructions, comprising the Content.

**Retailer Revenue:** the Revenue received by Whisk in respect of
Purchases.

**Retailer:** a retailer from whom Users can make Purchases.

**Revenue:** monies received in cleared funds by Whisk excluding any
amounts which, upon or after receipt, are payable by Whisk: (a) as VAT
or any other applicable sales tax; and (b) to a third party that is
entitled to a proportion of the said monies as a result of assisting
Whisk in agreeing terms with the relevant Retailer and/or Product Brand
Sponsor.

**Site:** the website (or websites) operated by the Licensor offering
the Content and/or on which the Widget will be embedded, as listed in
the Term Sheet.

**Term Sheet:** the form described as such, attached at the front of
this licence.

**Term:** the term of this licence as set out in clause 8.

**User Data:** all information relating to Users either collected
howsoever by Whisk or provided by Users when registering for or using
the Platform.

**User:** a user of the Platform.

**Widget Advertising Option:** the method for advertising through the
Widget set out in the Term Sheet.

**Widget:** that part of the Platform which is the embedded mechanism by
which adverts are displayed on the Site, and which includes the Button
linking Content on the Site to the Platform and allowing Purchases
through the Platform from the Site.
