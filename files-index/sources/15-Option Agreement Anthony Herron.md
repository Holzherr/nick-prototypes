Background

(A)       The Company has established the FOODIENT LTD EMI Share Option Plan (Plan) for the grant
          of share options to eligible employees of the Company. A share option granted under the Plan
          may or may not be intended to qualify as an Enterprise Management Incentives (EMI) share
          option, which is a kind of tax-favoured share option. The Option Holder is an employee of the
          Company.

(B)       The Company wishes to grant the Option Holder an option under the Plan, on the terms set out
          in this Agreement and subject to the rules of the Plan.

6.    Tax and national insurance contributions (NICs)

6.1   The Option Holder irrevocably agrees to:

      (a)     pay to the Company, his employer or former employer (as appropriate) the amount of
              any Tax Liability; or

      (b)     enter into arrangements to the satisfaction of the Company, his employer or former
              employer (as appropriate) for payment of any Tax Liability.


6.2   If:

      (a)     the Option Holder does not fulfil his obligations arising under clause 6.1(a) or clause
              6.1(b) in respect of any Tax Liability relating to the exercise of the Option within seven
              days after the date of exercise; and

      (b)     Option Shares are readily saleable at that time,


      the Company shall withhold Sufficient Shares from the Option Shares which would otherwise be
      delivered to the Option Holder. From the net proceeds of sale of those withheld Option Shares,
      the Company shall:

      (c)     retain (if the Company is to account for or pay the relevant Tax Liability); or

      (d)     pay to the Option Holder's employer or former employer (if that person is liable to
              account for or pay the relevant Tax Liability),


      an amount equal to the Tax Liability, and shall pay any balance to the Option Holder. The
      Option Holder's obligations under clause 6.1 shall not be affected by any failure of the Company
      to withhold Option Shares under this clause 6.2.

                                 Schedule 1 Relevant Restrictions

The Option Shares are subject to:


   1. Deemed transfer provisions contained in the Articles, which provide that in the event the Option
       Holder ceases to be an employee of the Company any Option Shares registered in the name of
       the Option Holder shall be subject to the deemed transfer provisions contained in article 7 of the
       Articles. The Company and the relevant existing shareholders have agreed in advance that the
       Option Holder shall be automatically considered a “Good Leaver” as set out in the Articles. As
       a Good Leaver the following provisions shall apply to the Option Holder in the event the
       deemed transfer provisions ever apply:


       •   article 7.4 of the Articles provides that as a Good Leaver the Option Holder will have the
           option of retaining their Option Shares provided they do not exceed 15% of the entire
           issued share capital of the Company;


       •   article 7.5 of the Articles stipulates that the Option Shares retained pursuant to article 7.4
           shall not carry any voting rights; and


       •   those Option Shares not retained pursuant to article 7.4 shall be subject to the deemed
           transfer provisions and the Option Holder shall be entitled to an amount equal to the open
           market value of the relevant number of their Option Shares.

   2. Pre-emption rights on the transfer of any Option Shares contained in article 6 of the Articles
       which provide, in short, that the Option Holder cannot transfer any Option Shares registered in
       the name of the Option Holder without first offering the same to the Company and the
       Company’s other shareholders.
