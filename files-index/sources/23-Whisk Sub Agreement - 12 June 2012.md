## RECITALS

A. This Agreement contains the terms upon which the Investors and the Private Investors are investing in the Company and provisions governing the future affairs of the Company which the parties have agreed to implement.

B. The principal activity of the Company is the provision of technology services in the field of food, drink and related recipes, online grocery retail and recipe publishing.

C. The purpose of the investment is to provide the Company with working capital and facilitate growth.

D. EALP has agreed to invest £24,996.50 in the Company on the terms set out in this Agreement.

E. BCC have agreed to invest £49,993.00 in the Company on the terms set out in this Agreement.

F. The Private Investors have agreed to invest up to an aggregate of £94,989.56 on Completion, in the Company on the terms set out in this Agreement.

G. EALP and the Fund Manager aim to be supportive investors in the Company, and help the Managers grow the Company profitably. The Company may need to raise more finance to achieve the Shareholders' joint objectives, and EALP and the Fund Manager confirm that they would be willing to consider providing further finance to the Company, subject to the satisfactory performance of the Company, the preparation and submission to the Fund Manager of a new investment proposal and with such proposal being approved by EALP's investment committee. In return, the Fund Manager expects the Managers to manage the business responsibly. In particular, monthly management information, and annual audited accounts, must be comprehensive, timely and accurate, and the payment of interest and dividends must be prompt.

H. The Managers and EALP aim to achieve a substantial capital gain from their shareholdings in the Company. The joint objective of the parties to this Agreement is to achieve a £25 million valuation of the Company by 31st May 2017.

I. EALP is partly funded by the European Regional Development Fund.
