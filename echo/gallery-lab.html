Skills gallery lab · pick one

More cards, at a glance.

Four ways to show the skills gallery in section 02 with several cards visible at once — keeping the premium glowing-card feel, losing the one-at-a-time limit. Pick the one you like and I'll wire it into the live page.

Same nine skill cards in every option. They differ only in how many you see and how you move through them.
Variant 1

Wide coverflow — five in view

The same coverflow you have, just tuned so five cards stay readable — the two on each side don't shrink away. Keeps the 3D depth and the centre-spotlight; drag or use the arrows. Closest to today, minimal change.

Variant 2

The Rail — a shelf you scroll

Cards line up full-size on a shelf; three-and-a-half show at once, the rest peek in from the edge. Drag or scroll sideways. The App-Store pattern — familiar, browsable, every card equally legible.

← drag / scroll to browse all nine →
Variant 3

The Fan — a hand of cards

All nine fanned in an arc like a hand of cards — every one visible at once, overlapping at the edges. Hover (or tap) lifts one to the front. The most "gallery", the most premium, the most playful.

Variant 4

The Wall — see everything

No gallery motion at all — every card laid out in a tidy grid, all nine in view, hover to highlight. Best if "browse the whole library at a glance" matters more than the spotlight drama.
